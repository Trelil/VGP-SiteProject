I'm vgperson, but you may call me vghime, Princess of the Vocaloid Kingdom. The v is lowercase. And the g. And the... you get the idea. I translate things, if you haven't noticed. I also do computery stuff and play video games. (Could one of these be related to my name? A mystery for the ages...)

My Tumblr has a bunch of translations and other things not on my website. My YouTube channel is mostly for subtitled songs. My SoundCloud (old account) has a bunch of songs I made in PXtone.

If you want to ask me something, you can ask me on Tumblr if you have one, or email vghimmel at gmail. Any other method of contact is playing a dangerous game (of probably never getting a reply).

Please don't send me requests for song translations. If I want to do a song, I'll come across it myself. I also try to regularly look for games to translate and have passed on a lot of candidates, so I generally won't accept requests for those either. However, if you find a particularly good game which you think I've overlooked, you can try suggesting it.
