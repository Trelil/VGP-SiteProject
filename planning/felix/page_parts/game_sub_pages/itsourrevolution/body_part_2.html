It's Our Revolution! is a freeware "puzzle RPG" by Wood Penguin made in RPG Maker VX Ace.

The goal of the game is for the unheard youth to gather a following to sway the king. Unfortunately, as your ensemble grows, you'll find it becomes a little difficult to get around...

At the start, don't bother recruiting anyone.
Get the Guild Guide, Chef Guide, Merchant Guide, and Scholar Guide.

Go between towns gathering a bunch of people.
Go to the castle jail and fight the soldiers, then get Kitt to learn how to open doors.
You'll then have to drop most of your party; don't worry about doing this when you have to.

Get the guides that require opening doors (Craftsman Guide and Servant Guide) and the Sickle.

Gather 100 people and go to the castle town adventurer's guild.
Get Arnold (the guildmaster), then all the adventurers.

From there, go to the dungeons and fight the bosses.
Restock on people (and get back boss recruits) as necessary, and get guides and items when able.
Remember, you can get all those adventurers at any time now!

Once you've beaten every boss, gather Sylph, Frau, Ifrit, and Dryad.
You should probably avoid recruiting anyone you don't need for ease of movement.
Once back on the world map, they unlock the Dark Lord's Castle.

After getting the Dark Lord, take him to the Pirate Hideout for the Goddess and the Soldier Guide.
Go to the ruins, into the left building, and destroy the object to open a warp to the Distant Somewhere, where you can refight enemies.
This is how you can get TP for Dark Lord's Teleport ability, which you must use exhaustively to get 999 people.

After that, it's just a matter of collecting as many members as you can, Teleporting out when you get stuck, and returning to the Distant Somewhere to refill on TP.
