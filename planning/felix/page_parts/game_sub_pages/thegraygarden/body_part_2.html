The Gray Garden is a pseudo-RPG with a heavy story focus, made by Mogeko (site name Deep-Sea Prisoner) in RPG Maker 2000.

The Gray Garden: a world where angels and demons live in harmony. Yosafire, a demon who likes flowers, enjoys a carefree life with her friends. Then one day, a mysterious bat appears in the garden...

Warning: There are a number of depictions of violence and gore throughout the game. However, none of it is presented in a "startling" way, and nothing too detailed is shown graphics-wise.

Extras

The game is very straightfoward, but there are some things you might miss. (Spoiler warning!)

Bad End 1

During the part in Flame Town where Emalf chases you, one of the wrong turns will lead to a dead end where he catches you.

Bad End 2

Lose the first fight against Poemi in the Wastelands.

Bad End 3

When Macarona finds Rawberry in the village, choose "Yaaaaaaaaaah!!!"

Normal End

Lose to the final boss (the actual fight).

Radio Wave

After beating the final boss, go to the coast to get this. It unlocks a thing in the bonus room.

Second Playthrough

There are a few minor bonuses on your second playthrough. Whether they're worth actually playing again for is... up to you, I suppose.

Apples

You don't need to collect any apples in Apple Park to proceed. Uh, thanks? ...How does that work?

666 Ticket

Froze's house will contain a 666 Ticket, which summons the Passing Demon to help in battle.

Prosciutto Equipment

A side area in Evening Forest formerly blocked by plants will be open. A Mogeko there will make Yosafire max level and give her the overpowered Prosciutto equipment.

???

The ??? video is different depending on if you view it right after beating the game the first time, or if you view it after returning the bonus room on a second playthrough. (Just starting a second loop and going to the door in Yosafire's house ASAP will work.)

Garden Gray The

Mogeko released Garden Gray The 0.00 for April Fools' Day 2014. (Mac version at same link) It's about ten minutes long and quite silly. And it shouldn't really spoil the main game at all.
