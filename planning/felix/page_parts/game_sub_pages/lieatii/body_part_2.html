LiEat II (a portmanteau of "lie" and "eat") is a short freeware RPG by △○□× (Miwashiba) made in WOLF RPG Editor. It is the sequel to LiEat. Playing the games in order is highly recommended.

A con artist travels with the dragon Efina in search of more information to sell. Led to Azure Town by rumors, "Hal" assumes the identity of a guard at the town's resort. And as they investigate, they discover what's going on behind the scenes...

This is a short story about an hour long at most. There are some battles, but they're not particularly difficult.

Walkthrough

A straightforward walkthrough. Click or highlight the black boxes to show spoiler text. It also explains where to get equipment.

Equipment

Equipment for Hal and Efina is hidden in a few places around the resort. You can get any of these basically anytime.

Weapons:

Serrated Knife and Small Tart, found in Hal and Efina's room.

Armor:

Big Liar and Dragon Aura, found in Lucas's room.

Overpowered Weapons:

Legendary Knife and Legendary Macaron, found in the bottom-right planter in the entrance hall.

Day 1

Talk to the guards and go inside. Take the right stairs, go to the middle room, and talk to the dealer. Go downstairs and into the bar, the room on the right. Talk to the man there and fight the lie. Go into the center door and walk toward the stage. Take the left stairs and go to your room on the far left. Take the right stairs, go to the far-right room, and talk to William. Go to the bar and talk to Lucas. Fight the lie outside, then return to your room. Wander around if you want, then sleep in the right bed in your room. Enter the center door downstairs and go up to the stage.

Day 2

Go to the far-right room on the east side and talk to Sofia. Return to your room and talk to Lucas, twice. Go to the far-right room on the east side and talk to Sofia. Go one room left from Sofia's and talk to Olivia. Go into the room next to yours and talk to Brett. Then talk to Neil. Talk to Hal in the entrance hall.

Go to Olivia's room and talk to William, then talk to him again outside. Fight the lie - it happened before he came - then talk to him. Go to Sofia's room and talk to her. Fight the lies - she's not worried, William is human - then talk to her. Go to Olivia's room and talk to her. Fight the lie - they don't fight - then talk to her. Go to the casino to scout out. Return to your room. Go to the casino to play. Talk to Olivia afterward. Check the door. Wake up Efina and fight both sets of lies. (You can return to your room and sleep in the bed to heal.)

Go to the entrance hall and check on the person in the middle. Go to Neil and Brett's room. Talk to Neil and check on William. (Lose to get the bad ending.)
