Mimicry Man is a freeware Mimic simulator by Kazusa Norakumo made in RPG Maker 2000. Ultimately, it's a puzzle game.

The Dark Lord has tasked Mimicry Man with defeating the Hero, just because. To lure him, he'll ultimately need to obtain the Solar Sword the Hero seeks. And Mimicry Man knows only one technique: show people what they want, then ambush!
