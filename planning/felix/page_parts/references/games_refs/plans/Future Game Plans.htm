Future Game Plans

The way I translate games, I don't have a "current project" the majority of the time - when I find a project I want to do, I'll just do it as quickly as possible.
The most information I can give about my future projects is telling you what the creators of the games I've done already are up to, as they're a likely source of new games I'll want to translate.
If an author isn't included here, it's probably because I either have no idea what they're up to, or have no plans to do more of their games in the future.

kouri
Ib: No apparent update plans.

kouri has wanted to do a new project for some time, but it's unclear what it will be; it may not even be a game.
It is highly unlikely it will be a sequel to Ib, and he doesn't seem to want to make another horror game.

Fummy
The Witch's House: No apparent update plans.

Fummy doesn't have a blog or anything beyond the TWH site, and I've seen nothing indicating plans for more games.

Miscreant's Room (Sen)
Misao: No apparent update plans.
Mad Father: No apparent update plans.

Sen might be working on a non-horror game, but almost nothing is known about it.
His Mad Father FAQ explicitly says there will be no direct sequel, though there is the possibility of a game in the same series.
Lastly, he has announced that there will be a Misao novel and a Mad Father novel coming out at roughly the same time, but no further details. I'll wait for more information before I say anything else.

Uri
Insanity: Not translating, as it's not a very good game, and Uri agrees. Summary here.
Paranoiac: No apparent update plans.
The Crooked Man: No apparent update plans.
Mermaid Swamp: No apparent update plans.
The Sandman: No apparent update plans.

Uri is hard at work on several games. The current release schedule is: The Boogie Man, Sudden Illness, Pedestal.
Other possible future titles (which were once on her site but are now in unknown status - for all I know, they became other games) are Haname and One Case.
Lastly, there is a fourth game planned for the Strange Men Series (and there could be more if Uri has ideas), but even I know nothing at all about it.

tachi
HeadLess: Not translating, as I find it kind of... dumb. If you're really curious, a summary is hidden on the Moonlight Ghost page.
Hello? Hell... o?: No apparent update plans.
Moonlight Ghost: No apparent update plans.
Hero & Daughter: Still updating regularly; more details on the page.

tachi will probably be updating Hero & Daughter for... a while.
It's currently unknown what his next project will be.

Miwashiba
Alice mare: No apparent update plans.
LiEat: No apparent update plans.
LiEat II: No apparent update plans.
LiEat III: No apparent update plans.

Miwashiba is currently working on 1bitHeart, which so far has mostly just had random screenshots and not much info. It is still a Wolf RPG game, but has a side perspective.
The LiEat series is complete. Alice mare will not have a sequel, as the true ending is, according to her, "the best happy ending in that world."

Deep-Sea Prisoner (Mogeko)
Mogeko Castle: No apparent update plans.
The Gray Garden: Idly remarked that (since the original art asserts were double-resolution) they might like to remake it in RPG Maker VX, but that it might be fine as it is.
Wadanohara and the Great Blue Sea: No apparent update plans.

Mogeko has "announced" at least three future games via the bonus rooms, and it's unclear what order they'll come in. They are Carnival Rhythm, The End of Wonderland, and Mogeko Castle Gaiden.
In addition, they want to make a prequel to The Gray Garden in the not-too-distant future, then a sequel to complete the trilogy.
Note that Ice Scream is a manga/interactive thing and Poison Bugs is a video series, so it's unlikely there would be games for them.

S-tele
Touhou Mother: No apparent update plans.

S-tele is still working on Return of Touhou Mother, but he finds it very difficult to write for its relatively serious plot, and thus progress is very slow. On Video 29, he said he wanted to wrap it up in 2014.
(Date of last video (29): May 12th, 2014. Date of last screenshot on Twitter: May 10th, 2014.)
In the meantime, he's been making other weird RPG Maker games. (I'm not likely to translate any of those.)
