Moonlight Ghost is a "reverse horror game" by tachi made in RPG Maker VX Ace.
This ends up making it a level-based high score arcade puzzle game. But not actually horror.

A reverse horror game, where you do the horror!
It's up to you, the player, to scare people!
It's a somewhat odd adventure game with four chapters in all.

Troubleshooting

Please make sure to extract the game to its own folder instead of running directly from the ZIP file.
If you don't do so, any saves made will be lost the next time you run the game.

tachi (Gives Art No. 22)

District 9 Block 6:
Spirit Photographer (Buy Art No. 23 and 24)

District 11 Block 1:
Hello? Hell... o? (Get No. 27 to 33 randomly from curtains)

HeadLess (Art No. 34 and 35)
